Interactive 3D Molecular Models

Rotate biochemical compounds in space with ball-and-stick, stick, sphere, and line views. Models load from PubChem 3D SDF conformers and render in the browser with 3Dmol.js.

Book Home Reaction Atlas Compound Formula Index Model List
These structures are single-molecule 3D conformers, usually computed or curated by PubChem. They are useful for seeing atoms, bonds, approximate geometry, and functional groups. They are not molecular-dynamics simulations, not cellular crowding models, and not a single permanent "physical state" for flexible biomolecules in water. Generic biochemical classes, such as "fatty acid" or "triacylglycerol", are represented by a named example molecule.
